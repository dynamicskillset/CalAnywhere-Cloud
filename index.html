Scheduling without the overhead

CalAnywhere creates scheduling pages that show only when you're free. Works with any iCal calendar. No email collected, no OAuth, no lock-in.

📅

Works with any calendar

Google, Outlook, Proton, Apple, Fastmail. If it has an iCal feed, it works. No integrations, no app permissions.

🔑

No email, no passwords

Sign in with a 3-emoji handle and your iCal URL. Nothing to leak, nothing to sell.

⏳

Pages expire

Each page has a configurable expiry. When it's gone, visitors see a clear message. Your calendar data is never stored.

How it works

  1. 1
    Sign up with an Emoji ID

    Pick a 3-emoji handle and paste your iCal URL. That's your account.

  2. 2
    Create a scheduling page

    Set your availability hours and timezone. DST is handled automatically.

  3. 3
    Share the link

    Visitors see only your free slots, never the details of your events.

  4. 4
    Get notified

    Someone requests a time. You get notified. That's it.
